About

International Festival of Yucatecan Music 2023

The festival takes its attendees on an enchanting journey through the soulful sounds of Yucatecan music. From traditional folk music that echoes the ancient traditions of the Maya civilization to contemporary fusions that reflect the region's modern creativity, the festival showcases the breadth and depth of Yucatán's musical expressions.
