Welcome to the

International Festival of Yucatecan Music 2023

The International Festival of Yucatecan Music is an annual celebration of the rich musical heritage of the Yucatán Peninsula. The festival brings together talented musicians, performers, and music enthusiasts from around the globe to immerse themselves in the captivating rhythms and melodies of Yucatecan culture.

2023.10.15(FRI)~28(MON)

@Gran Muse del Mundo Maya, Mérida, Yucatan

Main Program

Forum

Dive into the fascinating history and evolution of Yucatecan music, exploring its roots, influences, and significance in the region's cultural identity.

Film Forum

Gain insights into the lives of Yucatecan musicians, exploring their creative journeys and the impact of music on the community.

Roundtable Discussions

Discuss the importance of preserving traditional music and cultural practices, and explore innovative approaches to safeguarding Yucatecan musical heritage.

Concerts

Experience the enchanting sounds of traditional Yucatecan sones, jarana music and trova performed by local artists and folk ensembles.

Inmersive Experiences

Engage in interactive cultural experiences, where you can participate in traditional dance rituals and drumming sessions under the guidance of local experts.
